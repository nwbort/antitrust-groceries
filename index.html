About the Podcast

Welcome to Antitrust Grocery, the podcast that dives deep into the complex world of competition and monopoly issues within the grocery industry. Join us as we explore the impact of market concentration on consumers, suppliers, and the economy as a whole.

Latest Episode

Episode 1: Foodstuffs - A Closer Look

In our inaugural episode, we examine Foodstuffs, one of the major players in the grocery market in New Zealand. We discuss the proposed merger between the two island-based cooperatives.
